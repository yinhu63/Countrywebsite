About China

China is the world’s most populated country, with over 1.4 billion people. It has a rich history and beautiful landscapes, from snowy mountains to tropical forests. Famous landmarks like the Great Wall and the Terracotta Army are in China. The country is also known for its delicious food, such as dumplings, Peking duck, and hot pot. China has greatly influenced art, science, and philosophy and is the birthplace of Confucianism and Taoism.

Flag of China: The flag of China features a red background with five yellow stars in the top left corner. The large star represents the Communist Party, while the four smaller stars represent the four social classes in China. It symbolizes the unity of the Chinese people.

Map of China: China is located in East Asia and is bordered by 14 countries. It spans a vast area of over 9.5 million square kilometers, making it the third-largest country by area. The country's diverse geography includes deserts, mountains, plateaus, and fertile plains.
