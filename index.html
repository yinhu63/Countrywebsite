China

China is a large East Asian country with a population exceeding 1.4 billion, making it the world's second-most populous country and one of the oldest continuous civilizations. Officially called the People's Republic of China, it is a communist state with a market-based economy, a diverse landscape, and a rich history that has recently undergone rapid modernization. Its capital is Beijing, and its official language is Mandarin Chinese.
